About Our Project

A simple, smart solution to modern QR needs.

Purpose of the Website

Our website offers an all-in-one toolkit for generating QR codes, shortening URLs, collecting feedback, and more. It is designed to be fast, responsive, and easy to use—whether you're a student, developer, or business owner.

Vision & Technology

Our goal is to provide seamless digital tools powered by modern web technologies. We focus on user experience, simplicity, and practicality. The tech stack includes:

  • HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5
  • JavaScript for interactivity
  • Python (Flask) for backend logic
  • MongoDB/MySQL for data storage
